About Me

Hello! I'm Aaryan Patel

I'm an analytically driven 2nd-year student at the University of Toronto, majoring in Economics with Data Analytics and minoring in Computer Science & Statistics. I'm passionate about applying quantitative analysis and programming to complex AI challenges, particularly in developing responsible, multimodal AI and enhancing operational efficiency.
