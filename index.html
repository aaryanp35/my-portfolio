About Me

Hello! I'm Aaryan Patel

I'm an analytically driven 2nd-year student at the University of Toronto, majoring in Economics with Data Analytics and minoring in Computer Science & Statistics. I'm passionate about applying quantitative analysis and programming to complex AI challenges, particularly in developing responsible, multimodal AI and enhancing operational efficiency.

Currently, I'm engaged in AI research under Geoffrey Hinton's team (Google Fellow), investigating techniques to accelerate neural tree learning to reduce computational requirements and capital infrastructure spend. This work combines my interests in machine learning, data analysis, and practical applications of AI.

Beyond academics, I'm a collegiate athlete on the University of Toronto Rowing Team and was selected for the U.S. Rowing Olympic Development Program Trials. I'm also passionate about STEM outreach, having developed and delivered computer literacy and coding lessons to diverse communities.
